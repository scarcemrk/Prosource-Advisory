Who We Are

Prosource Advisory Pvt. Ltd. is an approved training partner of NSDC, Skill India, and HSSC. We run training centers in Ahmedabad and Porbandar, offering high-quality healthcare training. Our General Duty Assistant (GDA) course prepares students to support medical professionals and care for patients in healthcare settings across India.

Our Story

We were working for B to B agreement for caregiver between our government and Israel since 1996, under which we have successfully sent more than 5000 caregivers to the Israel. Over the years, we’ve successfully helped countless caregivers to launch their careers in Israel. Prosource Advisory Pvt. Ltd. was established in 2024, with the vision of offering accessible and high-quality training for individuals eager to pursue a career in healthcare.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower individuals with the practical skills and industry knowledge needed to excel as General Duty Assistants in the healthcare sector. We strive to ensure that every student is prepared to meet the demands of a dynamic and essential healthcare career while upholding the highest standards of care, ethics, and professionalism.

Our Vision

To be a leading provider of healthcare training, recognized for excellence in education, hands-on experience, and the successful placement of our graduates in meaningful healthcare roles. We aim to contribute to improving patient care standards by equipping professionals with the skills and compassion necessary to thrive in the healthcare industry.
