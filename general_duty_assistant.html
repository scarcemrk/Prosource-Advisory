General Duty Assistant

Welcome to the General Duty Assistant (GDA) Program, a comprehensive training course designed to equip you with the essential skills required to excel as a healthcare assistant. Whether you're interested in working in hospitals, clinics, nursing homes, or other healthcare settings, this program will prepare you to play a key role in patient care and support the healthcare team.

What is a General Duty Assistant?

  • A General Duty Assistant (GDA) plays a vital role in the healthcare system. GDAs support doctors, nurses, and medical teams by assisting patients with daily needs, ensuring their comfort, and maintaining a clean and safe environment.

Key Duties of a GDA

  • Assist with daily activities (bathing, dressing, eating)
  • Support during procedures & monitor vital signs
  • Maintain patient records and report health changes
  • Ensure hygiene and sanitation in the facility
  • Help with mobility and rehabilitation

Program Highlights

  • Comprehensive hands-on training
  • Industry-recognized certification
  • Career-ready skill development

Course Overview

The General Duty Assistant (GDA) program offers practical and theoretical training for individuals interested in entering the healthcare sector. The course is designed to provide students with a thorough understanding of healthcare fundamentals, patient care techniques, hygiene, and basic medical procedures. Upon completion, graduates will be well-equipped to support healthcare professionals in delivering quality care to patients.

In this course, students will

  • Gain practical knowledge of basic healthcare skills and patient care techniques.
  • Learn how to assist doctors, nurses, and medical staff in day-to-day operations.
  • Understand the importance of hygiene, infection control, and maintaining a safe environment for patients.
  • Develop excellent communication and interpersonal skills, crucial for working in healthcare settings.

Course Details

  • Duration: 6 Months
  • Mode: Offline
  • Certification: Accredited Certificate upon Completion
